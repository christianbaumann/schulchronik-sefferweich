### Historical Explanations

*   **Disputed Election:** Two different lists of elected officials are presented. The Kreisausschuss (district committee) was to decide the dispute.
*   **Local Governance:** Election of Dasbach Heinrich as Ortsvorsteher (village head).
*   **School Governance:** Elections for Elternbeirat and Schulvorstand.
