### Historical Explanations

*   **Occupation of the Ruhr and Rhineland:** The entry from late March refers to the occupation of the Ruhr industrial area by French and Belgian troops.
*   **Reichstag Election of May 4, 1924:** The entry for May 6 describes the German federal election held two days prior.
*   **Zentrum Party (Centre Party):** This was a major Catholic political party in Germany.
*   **"Ewiges Gebet" (Perpetual Adoration):** This is a Catholic devotional practice.
